Reasons to visit Nepal




Lake Tilicho

Tilicho Lake was once known as Nepal’s highest lake, as it is situated at an altitude of 4,919 meters (16,138 ft)

Rhododendron

It is the national flower of Nepal

One horned rhino

The greater one-horned rhino is restricted to less than a dozen protected areas, scattered across north and north-eastern India and southern Nepal.
